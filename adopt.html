Adopt a Pet

Adoption Conditions:

  • Adopters must be at least 18 years old.
  • A valid ID with current address is required.
  • Adopters must agree to a home visit by our team.
  • All current pets in the home must be spayed/neutered and up to date on vaccinations.
  • Adopters must agree to provide proper care and a loving home for the pet.

Adoption Fee: $50
